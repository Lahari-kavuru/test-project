Explore the Grand Canyon State !!

Arizona, formerly part of territory of New Mexico, was formed a seperate territory in 1863. Later in 1912, Arizona became a state.
Arizona is most popular for Grand Canyon.
It is known for Copper mineral which is found abundantly in that state
Arizona is an iconic travel destination. Some of the most visited places in Arizona are:

Grand Canyon National Park

A majestic natural wonder showcasing the awe-inspiring beauty of one of the world's most iconic and expansive canyons.

Saguaro National Park

Home to the towering saguaro cacti, this desert oasis preserves the unique and vibrant ecosystem of the American Southwest.

Havasu Falls

A breathtaking turquoise waterfall oasis nestled within the Grand Canyon, known for its striking beauty and remote location in the Havasupai Indian Reservation.
