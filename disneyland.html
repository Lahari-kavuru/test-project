Disneyland California, often referred to simply as Disneyland, is a world-famous theme park located in Anaheim, California.
Opened in 1955 by Walt Disney himself, it was the first-ever Disneyland park and has since become an iconic destination for families and Disney enthusiasts worldwide.
The park is divided into two main sections: Disneyland Park, also known as Disneyland Resort, and Disney California Adventure Park,
both offering a wide array of attractions, entertainment, and dining options.
From classic rides like Space Mountain and the Matterhorn to beloved characters and immersive lands like Fantasyland and Star Wars:
Galaxy's Edge, Disneyland California continues to enchant visitors with the magic of Disney storytelling.

Disney California Adventure Park

A dynamic and immersive theme park adjacent to Disneyland, celebrating the spirit of California with thrilling attractions, entertainment, and culinary experiences.

Downtown Disney District

An vibrant and entertainment-packed shopping and dining district at the Disneyland Resort in California, offering a diverse range of experiences and Disney-themed merchandise.
