Discover the Golden State !!

California, located on the western coast of the United States, has a rich and diverse history.
In 1821, Mexico gained independence from Spain, and California became a Mexican territory.
The discovery of gold in the Sierra Nevada foothills in 1848 led to the California Gold Rush, which brought a massive influx of settlers and transformed California into a state in 1850.
It has been a hub for the entertainment industry, technological advancements in Silicon Valley, and a leader in environmental conservation and policy.
Today, California is the most populous state in the U.S. and continues to be a global economic and cultural powerhouse.

Yosemite National Park

A pristine wilderness of towering granite cliffs, majestic waterfalls, and ancient sequoia groves, renowned for its natural beauty and outdoor recreational opportunities.

Lake Tahoe

A stunning alpine lake nestled in the Sierra Nevada mountains, known for its crystal-clear waters, year-round outdoor activities, and picturesque landscapes.

Disney Land

The iconic theme park where dreams come to life, offering magical attractions, beloved characters, and unforgettable family experiences.
