Walt Disney World



The Walt Disney World Resort, also called Walt Disney World or Disney World, or simply abbreviated WDW, is an entertainment resort complex in Bay Lake and Lake Buena Vista, Florida, United States.It is a place where dreams come to life, where enchantment and wonder are around every corner, and where cherished memories are made.

Nearby Places

Epcot

Epcot is one of Disney's theme parks, located right next to Walt Disney World. It offers a unique experience, with a focus on technology, culture, and international cuisine. Don't miss the iconic Spaceship Earth attraction.

Disney's Hollywood Studios

It is located within the Walt Disney World Resort, this park celebrates the entertainment industry. You can experience attractions like Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge, Toy Story Land, and the Tower of Terror.

SeaWorld Orlando

Located a short drive from Walt Disney World, SeaWorld offers marine life exhibits, thrilling rides, and entertaining shows featuring dolphins, killer whales, and more.
